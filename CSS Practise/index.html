Popcorn

Popcorn made out of maize. It is a favourite snack of money people.It is eaten and enjoyed while watching movies or TV.

Preparation

Toke few maize grains. Put them in cooker and heat the cooker when the temperature reaches high the grains turn into popcorn in a popping fashion after that add a masala of your choice.
